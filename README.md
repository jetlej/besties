# 💬 Besties

A little macOS app that reads your iMessage history and reminds you to text the people you care about 💛

Ever had a friend you used to talk to all the time, and then life happened? Besties finds those conversations and gently nudges you to reconnect.

## What it does

- Reads your iMessage history (locally, nothing leaves your Mac)
- **Reconnect** — scores your dormant conversations and shows you who you should probably text back ✨
- **All Time** — see who your top people are, ranked by total messages sent 👯

## Run it

1. Open `Besties/Besties.xcodeproj` in Xcode
2. Select your team under Signing & Capabilities
3. Build & Run
4. Grant Full Disk Access when prompted
